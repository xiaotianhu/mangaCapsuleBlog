From the start, our vision was to create a comic reader that's "not just functional, but genuinely delightful." After releasing version 1.0, we received extensive user feedback, with the most common pain points being: **lengthy decompression wait times for large archives** and **image quality issues with older comics**.

## Streaming Reading: Goodbye to Loading

Traditional comic apps typically require downloading and fully decompressing ZIP/RAR files locally. For a 2GB collection, this could mean waiting several minutes.

In version 2.0, we've completely rewritten the underlying file handling logic, implementing **Archive Streaming** technology. Now, regardless of file size, you can open files instantly with a single click. The system prioritizes loading data blocks for the current page, achieving "decompression while reading."

> "This is the fastest app I've used for opening 4GB archives from my NAS, no question." — Early beta user

## AI Image Enhancement: Giving Old Comics New Life

Many classic manga resources are stuck at 800px resolution, appearing blurry on modern Retina iPads. We've integrated a CoreML-based Super Resolution model.

Unlike cloud-based solutions, our AI processing runs entirely on-device:

- **Privacy Security**: Your images never get uploaded to any server.
- **Zero Cost**: No expensive GPU cloud service fees required.
- **Offline Available**: Enjoy HD quality anywhere, whether on a plane or in the subway.

## What's Next

This is just the beginning. In upcoming versions, we plan to introduce a "social bookshelf" feature, making it easier to share your collection preferences.
