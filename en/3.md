WebDAV is currently one of the file transfer protocols with the best compatibility and most balanced performance. Combined with Manga Capsule, you can read manga stored on your home NAS while slacking off at work (just kidding).

## Preparation

First, make sure your NAS has WebDAV service enabled and that you've configured port forwarding on your router (if accessing from outside your network).

```
// Typical WebDAV URL structure
http://[your domain or IP]:[port]/[shared folder path]
```

On the "Connection" page in Manga Capsule, select WebDAV and enter the information above. If the connection is successful, you should immediately see the file list.
