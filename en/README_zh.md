# Manga Capsule - The Ultimate Local Manga Reading Experience
[English](README.md) | [简体中文](README_zh.md)

![Manga Capsule Hero](./hero-3in1.png)

Manga Capsule is a local manga reader deeply customized for iOS/iPadOS. We're dedicated to solving the challenge of elegantly managing and reading massive local manga collections, using cutting-edge AI technology and exceptional interaction design to create a pure, efficient digital bookshelf for manga enthusiasts.

---

## 💎 Why Choose Manga Capsule?

### 1. 🎨 End-to-End AI High-Definition
Many treasured old manga have very low resolution, appearing blurry and full of noise on modern high-definition screens.
- **Real-time Processing**: Built-in deeply optimized AI high-definition algorithm requires no preprocessing, completing 2x-4x magnification and noise reduction instantly during page turning.
- **Quality Leap**: Effectively removes JPG compression artifacts, sharpens lines, presenting low-resolution manga with a "remastered" high-definition visual experience.
- **Fully Local**: All AI computations are completed locally on the device (leveraging Apple Neural Engine acceleration), no cloud uploads, privacy-protected with no data consumption.

### 2. 🔌 Multi-Protocol Remote Mounting & Sync
No longer limited by mobile device storage capacity.
- **NAS Enthusiast's Dream**: Deep support for **WebDAV** and **SMB** protocols, you can directly read massive resources from NAS or personal servers, experiencing smooth performance like local files.
- **Full Cloud Drive Support**: One-click mount for **Baidu Cloud**, **Aliyun Drive**, **Google Drive**, **Dropbox**, and more — cloud manga available on demand.
- **Lightning Sync**: Supports Wi-Fi AirDrop, system file sharing, iCloud Drive import.
- **Komga Support**: Supports OPDS-based Komga server, making manga management even more convenient.

### 3. 📦 All-Encompassing Format Support
Whether it's ancient archives or modern e-books, we've got you covered.
- **Archives**: ZIP, CBZ, RAR, CBR
- **E-books**: EPUB, MOBI, AZW3, PDF.
- **Image Formats**: JPG, PNG, WEBP, HEIC, GIF, and more.

### 4. 📖 Interaction Design Built for "Immersion"
- **Smart Edge Detection**: Automatically identifies and crops white/black margins in scanned manga, maximizing screen display area.
- **High-Speed Rendering**: Self-developed slice rendering engine enables instant opening and smooth page turning, even for large files containing thousands of ultra-clear original images.
- **Multiple Modes**: Supports horizontal double-page spread (restoring physical book experience), vertical long-form manga, Japanese/American manga reading order switching.
- **Exquisite Bookshelf**: Automatically extracts covers, supports multi-level folder classification management, creating your personal anime wall.
