## 2. Recommended Resource Formats

### 🎉 Recommended: epub、mobi、zip、cbz

### 🤔 Average: pdf

### 🙅 Not Recommended: rar、cbr、jpg、png、7z

**Reasons:**

- **epub and mobi** have page order controlled by code, offering better controllability, faster loading, and relatively higher resource quality. They support streaming. Covers are individually processed for optimal aesthetics.

- **zip** packages have a simple format, essentially equivalent to folders, with fast loading and relatively good quality. They support streaming. Cover aesthetics require adaptation and manual adjustment.

- **pdf** has many variations and complex rendering methods. While it's a universal format that can open anything, manga PDFs are essentially image collections that are performance-intensive, especially when using AI upscaling—they consume significant memory and battery. Quality is inconsistent and often poorly optimized.

- **rar and 7z** are legacy compression formats from ancient times. Resources are scarce, some are encrypted, generally highly compressed, and don't support streaming reading well.

- **cbz and cbr** are essentially zip and rar—foreigners originally used these extensions to distinguish eBooks from manga, forcing readers to determine file type by extension, enabling comic reader functionality in general-purpose readers.

- **jpg and png** folder image packages are cumbersome to manage, similar to photo albums. On a bookshelf, they appear as thumbnails—ugly.

## 5. Resource Source Sharing

### 0、漫画补档 Forum (The Legend That Disappeared)

**Recommended: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐**

Must mention this already-defunct forum with excellent resource quality and various scan masters. The rar and zip packages with two pages per image currently circulating on the market basically all originated from here. It was once my frequently used forum with strict access controls—some resources required a certain level to download. However, leveling up is no longer possible as the admin seems to have abandoned the forum. Latest status: shut down, became a legend.

### 1、Kmoe (Currently T0)

**Recommended: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐**

- https://kxo.moe/
- https://kxx.moe/

(URLs change frequently; if失效 means domain changed, just Google the keywords. Username and password are the same)

My most frequently used one. Complete resources, many versions. Some serialized manga quality is poor, but sufficient for reading finished works. Primarily epub and mobi formats. Has a free tier; paid experience is more enjoyable, but prices have increased significantly. Some resources require 🪜 to access.

### 2、Acgndog Anime

**Recommended: ⭐⭐⭐⭐**

https://www.acgndog.com/

Has various disk resources in large quantities, but not necessarily complete. Some resources are 7z-packed, some are png and jpg image packages, some are encrypted and require computer extraction. But the fact that it's free is remarkable. Not sure if there are paid items—at least it appears free.

### 3、Comicores

**Recommended: ⭐⭐⭐**

https://www.comicores.com/

The admin of this site seems very passionate. Many manga wide-page restorations, HD versions, and full-color versions are handcrafted and carefully selected. Downside is the 7z format—downloading gives a large compressed file that requires password extraction, then repackaging into files readable by various readers. Complex process. Recommended for those with computers; not recommended for those without.

### 4、Manhuazi

**Recommended: ⭐⭐⭐**

https://mhx12.com/

Feels like a shell site scraped from Kmoe. Haven't paid for the "faith" tier, so can't fairly judge. Has free quota, some manga updates faster than Kmoe, some slower. Also primarily epub and mobi formats.

### 5、cc-pdf

**Recommended: ⭐⭐⭐**

http://www.eccpdf.com/

Used this site a long time ago. Purchased lifetime membership. Has many vintage manga from early era, initially only in PDF, only supported Baidu Pan. Now has some Quark resources. To avoid being taken down by cloud drives, all filenames have been modified with homophones—OCD users need to fix on computer. Admin's server config is very low, site is extremely slow, domain changes frequently.

### 6、z-library

**Recommended: ⭐⭐**

Old established player, great for publications, but manga resource quantity and quality are poor. Many bloggers who don't even read manga recommend it as a manga download site.

### 8、Know Manga Backup

**Recommended: ⭐**

https://kindle.smgzd.com/

Unofficial backup site of 漫画补档 Forum. Registration costs 50R, not recommended.

### 9、Bu Yi Zhi Huo

**Recommended: ⭐**

https://www.byzhihuo.com/

Primarily a 3D model resource site with token system, only has a manga section. Used it before, but the site is quite old and resources aren't abundant. Strengths are some special niche books in American and European comics, plus many art books. PDF format, primarily Baidu Pan. After downloading, various encryption operations—very tedious. Resources are basically搬运党 (reuploaders) earning points, not carefully curated. Expensive—unless absolutely necessary, not recommended.
