## Manga Capsule version 1.31 is now available~

We've done something big, folks!  
The reader now supports "Split Large Images". You can split horizontal images into pages when viewing on your phone, or when you hold your iPad vertically~  
It's not enabled by default yet. When you encounter a book that can be split, a prompt will appear. You can enable it in the reader settings menu.  

#### Other updates:  
- The resource guide was previously banned, links have been updated  
- Page-turning simulation no longer freezes with books that have many pages  
- Reader single/double column mode detection strategy optimized  
- Fixed bookshelf loading animation issues  
- Fixed Baidu Netdisk not refreshing new folders  
- You can now right-click on Mac to open comics through Capsule, or simply drag a book directly into it
