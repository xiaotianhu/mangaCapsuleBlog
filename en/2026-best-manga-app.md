#### Streaming Reading
For the perfect reading experience, and to save iPads with 64GB of storage, we put our biggest effort into implementing "streaming reading." Streaming reading is like watching video — after opening and waiting a moment for some content to buffer, you can start reading normally, instead of waiting for everything to download locally. This greatly improves the reading experience and reduces wait time. Manga Capsule has implemented streaming reading for most formats. Compared to other comic readers that only support download-based reading, this is a core体现ation of the software's technical strength.

![流式阅读](https://mangacapsule.com/images/stream-load.jpg)

#### Vertical Comic Friendly
With the rise of Chinese and Korean webcomics, more and more people are reading them. Unlike Japanese manga, these two types are often displayed vertically as "webtoons," and many readers have poor support for them, with incorrect aspect ratios. Manga Capsule has done automatic optimization for this situation, making the display of vertical comics more friendly — you can start reading perfectly without any extra settings.

#### AI HD
Manga Capsule supports on-device AI HD, using a carefully tuned Waifu2X super-resolution model to enhance image detail. In plain English: it allows low-quality, blurry comics to be refined and upscaled through a model that runs directly on your device. No network traffic needed, yet it provides a better reading experience. Compatibility with older devices is also great — I tested it on older devices like iPad Mini5 and iPad Air3, and the results were very good.
